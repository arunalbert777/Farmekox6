# **App Name**: Farmekox

## Core Features:

- User-Friendly Registration: Easy registration process for farmers with minimal digital skills, providing quick access to the system.
- Season-wise Crop Recommendations: Intelligent, location-specific crop recommendations based on season and climate. Powered by generative AI and external weather data, providing farmers the right choice.
- Crop Calendar: Calendar view with sowing, irrigation, fertilization, and harvesting schedules.
- 7-Day Local Weather Forecast: Accurate, location-specific weather forecasts integrated with a refresh button and the Google API.
- Rain Alerts and Irrigation Advice: Notifications about upcoming rainfall with advice to skip irrigation. The LLM reasons whether to give irrigation advice based on the probability of rain being above a specified threshold; uses weather forecast as a tool.
- Fertilizer Dose Calculator: Barcode-based fertilizer identification with nutrient composition and product details retrieved from an external fertilizer database.
- AI Voice and Chat Advisory: AI-based voice and chat assistant providing farming guidance in Kannada and English using the Gemini API.
- News and Mandi Price Updates: Provide real-time agricultural news and mandi price updates.
- Ask Expert: Direct connection to agricultural experts for personalized advice, featuring local experts.
- Nearby Resources Locator: Location of nearby resources (seed stores, fertilizer shops, mandis) using the Google Maps API, with farm navigation.
- Multi-Language Interface: Support for multiple regional languages with a toggle button to switch between Kannada and English.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated orange (#E67E22) to represent the earth and harvest, reflecting the agricultural focus. 
- Background color: Desaturated light orange (#F8EFEA), creating a warm, inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Analogous yellow (#D4AC0D) to highlight important information and CTAs, enhancing user experience.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' sans-serif for headlines. Body font: 'Alegreya' serif for a balance of readability and style.
- Use clear, minimalist icons to represent various farming activities, resources, and weather conditions.
- Clean and spacious layout with a focus on intuitive navigation, ensuring easy access to key features.
- Subtle animations for loading states and transitions to provide a smooth and engaging user experience.
